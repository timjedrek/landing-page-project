FBO Services

Barrowman Municipal Airport (KOMG) serves the businesses and residents of Barrowman City and neighboring Counties. The airport’s modern infrastructure, served by an Instrument Landing System equipped 6500’ by 150’ main runway, makes it ideal for many aeronautical activities. Cargo, corporate, charter, and recreational flying, as well as fixed wing and helicopter flight training, occurs daily at KOMG. Jet fuel and AVGAS is available as well as hanger rentals. Come land here and see what all the chatter is about!

Our Mission

The Mission of the Barrowman Municipal Airport (KOMG) shall be to provide a safe, efficient, dependable and attractive first class public facility that professionally serves the greater Barrowman City Area. The airport shall strive to maintain its role as the major air transportation facility for the Barrowman City region, while continuing to be a responsive and responsible neighbor to the surrounding community.

-Barrowman Municipal Airport Management
